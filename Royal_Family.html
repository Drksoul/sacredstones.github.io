Eirika: Restoration Lady

She is the daughter of King Fado of Renais,
and the younger twin sister of Prince Ephraim.
She is the princess of Renais.

Ephraim: Restoration Lord

He is the son of King Fado of Renais,
and the older twin brother of Princess Eirika.
He is the crown prince of Renais.
